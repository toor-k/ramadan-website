Eating Times

Fasting begins at dawn and ends at sunset. Iftar is served at sunset, and Suhur should be taken before dawn.

Suhur

Suhur is the pre-dawn meal that provides energy for the day. It is recommended to have a balanced meal rich in protein and complex carbohydrates.
